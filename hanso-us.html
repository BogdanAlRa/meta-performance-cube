How This Dashboard Was Built

1
Weekly Chunk Extraction
Meta’s Marketing API was queried in 7-day windows across the full date range to stay within rate limits. Each account required ~112 API calls per breakdown slice.
2
4 Parallel Breakdown Slices
Meta blocks cross-group breakdowns, so we pull 4 slices independently: Demographics (age × gender), Delivery (platform × position × device), Geography (country), and Totals (no breakdown) — all at ad-level daily granularity.
3
N-Dimensional Cube Reconstruction
Slices are joined on ad_id × date to reconstruct the full 6-dimensional cube (Age × Gender × Platform × Position × Device × Country). Cross-cell estimates use proportional spend allocation.
4
Analysis & Attribution
15 metrics computed per cell: Spend, Revenue, ROAS, CPA, AOV, CPM, CPC, CTR, Frequency, Impressions, Clicks, Purchases, and funnel events. Backend attribution from Adtribute correlated across 9 models.
